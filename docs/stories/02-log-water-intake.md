# User Story: 2 - Log Water Intake

**As a** health-conscious user,
**I want** to easily log my water intake throughout the day,
**so that** I can stay properly hydrated and meet my daily hydration goals.

## Acceptance Criteria

* Simple tap interface to log water consumption
* Default serving sizes available (e.g., glass, bottle)
* Daily progress towards 8 glasses of water goal shown visually
* Quick access to water logging from dashboard

## Notes

* Target: 8 glasses of water daily
* Should be one of the simplest interactions in the app
* Part of MVP core functionality
