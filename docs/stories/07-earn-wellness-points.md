# User Story: 7 - Earn Wellness Points

**As a** health-conscious user,
**I want** to earn points for completing daily wellness goals,
**so that** I feel motivated and rewarded for maintaining healthy habits.

## Acceptance Criteria

* Points awarded for completing steps, water intake, sleep, and meditation goals
* Point values are clearly displayed when earned
* Total point balance is visible and tracked over time
* Point system rewards consistency over perfection

## Notes

* Gamification mechanic to increase engagement
* Should motivate without becoming overwhelming
* Foundation for broader achievement and reward system
