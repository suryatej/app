# User Story: 8 - Unlock Achievement Levels

**As a** health-conscious user,
**I want** to unlock achievement levels and badges for consistent wellness habits,
**so that** I can feel a sense of progression and accomplishment in my health journey.

## Acceptance Criteria

* Achievement levels include titles like "Wellness Warrior," "Hydration Hero," "Sleep Champion"
* Badges are awarded for specific milestones and consistent behavior
* Each level unlocks new features or customization options
* 7-day streaks are valued more highly than single perfect days

## Notes

* Rewards consistency over perfection to promote healthy long-term habits
* Achievement system should celebrate progress, not just perfect adherence
* Unlocked features provide tangible value for progression
