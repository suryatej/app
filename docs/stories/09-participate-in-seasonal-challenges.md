# User Story: 9 - Participate in Seasonal Challenges

**As a** health-conscious user,
**I want** to participate in time-limited seasonal wellness challenges,
**so that** I can stay engaged and motivated with fresh goals throughout the year.

## Acceptance Criteria

* Seasonal challenges are offered (e.g., "Summer Step Challenge," "Mindful March")
* Challenges have clear goals, timeframes, and participation tracking
* Progress towards challenge completion is visible
* Challenges provide variety while supporting core wellness habits

## Notes

* Time-limited events help maintain high engagement
* Should align with and reinforce core wellness behaviors
* Provides fresh content and motivation beyond daily tracking
