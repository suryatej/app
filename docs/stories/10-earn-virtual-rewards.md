# User Story: 10 - Earn Virtual Rewards

**As a** health-conscious user,
**I want** to earn virtual currency to unlock app customizations and premium features,
**so that** I can personalize my experience and access enhanced functionality through healthy behaviors.

## Acceptance Criteria

* Users earn gems or coins through wellness activities and achievements
* Virtual currency can be spent on app themes, meditation backgrounds, or customization options
* Free users can temporarily unlock premium content as rewards
* Reward system encourages continued engagement and healthy habits

## Notes

* Provides free users a taste of premium features
* Could drive premium conversions while maintaining engagement
* Virtual rewards should feel meaningful and valuable
