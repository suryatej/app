# User Story: 11 - Connect with Friends for Challenges

**As a** health-conscious user,
**I want** to connect with friends and participate in wellness challenges together,
**so that** I can stay motivated through social accountability and friendly competition.

## Acceptance Criteria

* Users can opt into social features while maintaining privacy-first approach
* Friend challenges can be created and joined
* Leaderboards show progress among connected friends
* Achievement sharing is available for those who opt in

## Notes

* Privacy-first implementation - social features are opt-in only
* Social accountability can significantly boost motivation and engagement
* Should support both competition and collaboration aspects
