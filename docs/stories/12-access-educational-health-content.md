# User Story: 12 - Access Educational Health Content

**As a** health-conscious user,
**I want** to access educational tips and information about wellness topics,
**so that** I can better understand the importance of healthy habits and how to improve them.

## Acceptance Criteria

* Educational content covers movement benefits, hydration importance, sleep hygiene basics
* Tips are presented in digestible, actionable formats
* Content is integrated contextually with relevant tracking features
* Information is evidence-based and health-expert reviewed

## Notes

* Content guidelines to be drafted by health expert (Sky)
* Should educate users on the 'why' behind wellness recommendations
* Helps users make informed decisions about their health habits
