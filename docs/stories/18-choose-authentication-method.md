# User Story: 18 - Choose Authentication Method

**As a** new or returning user,
**I want** to see a clear welcome screen with options to sign in or register via email or social media,
**so that** I can choose my preferred authentication method and quickly access the app.

## Acceptance Criteria

* Welcome/authentication screen presents clear options for sign in vs. register
* Both email and social media authentication options are prominently displayed
* UI clearly distinguishes between registration and login flows
* Design is clean, welcoming, and aligned with app branding
* Easy navigation between sign in and register screens

## Notes

* First impression for new users - must be intuitive and welcoming
* Should align with "Your wellness companion in one app" messaging
* Target audience values simplicity and quick onboarding
